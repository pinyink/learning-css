How CleanTrack Works

Our simple 4-step process makes managing cleaning schedules effortless and efficient.

Step 1

Asset Inventory Setup

Create your digital inventory of all assets that require regular cleaning. Categorize by type, location, and priority level. Upload photos and detailed specifications if needed.

Step 2

Schedule Configuration

Define cleaning frequencies for each asset or asset group. Set up standard operating procedures and checklists for different cleaning tasks. Assign responsibilities to team members.

Step 3

Task Execution

Staff receive automated notifications for upcoming cleaning tasks. They can access detailed instructions, checklists, and report completion with photos or notes directly from their mobile devices.

Step 4

Monitoring & Reporting

Supervisors monitor compliance in real-time. Generate reports for management reviews, compliance audits, and continuous improvement initiatives.
