Our Story

Since 1995, serving the freshest seafood in town

Ocean Catch was founded with a simple mission: to bring the freshest seafood from the ocean to your plate. Our founder, a third-generation fisherman, wanted to share his passion for quality seafood with the community.

Today, we continue that tradition by sourcing only the highest quality, sustainably caught seafood from local fishermen and trusted suppliers around the world.
